Session Information

Experience outdoor climbing at TQ Climb, the climbing facility at Torquay Boys' Grammar School. Sessions are 90 minutes long and suitable for complete beginners and experienced climbers alike, with access to our 7.5m auto-belay climbing wall and outdoor bouldering wall, with all climbing equipment provided.

Participant Requirements

  • Ages 8+
  • Minimum weight: 10kg
  • Maximum weight: 110kg

Parent & Guardian Requirements

  • Participants aged under 16 must have a parent or guardian remain on site throughout the session to assist with clipping.
  • Participants aged 16–18 must have a parent or guardian be contactable throughout the session.

Before You Arrive

  • Arrive 10–15 minutes before your session start time.
  • Ensure your waiver has been completed.
  • Wear comfortable clothing suitable for physical activity.

Footwear

Climbing shoes are recommended but not required. Suitable trainers are perfectly acceptable for most climbers and routes.


Open-toed footwear, sandals, sliders, flip-flops and Crocs are not permitted.

Equipment

All climbing equipment is provided. Personal climbing equipment may not be used during public sessions.
